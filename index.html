01

Overview

This App is a restaurant ordering and information platform. It uses an embedded WebView to deliver our restaurant's web interface, Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications, Cloud Firestore for server-side operational data, a local Room database to store your notification history on-device, and a Splash Screen for branding on launch.

Our overall approach is minimal data collection. We do not build user profiles, serve advertisements, or sell data to any third party.

03

What We Explicitly Do Not Store

To be completely clear, the following is never collected or retained by our App or servers:

  • Phone numbers — passed through during ordering but never written to any database
  • GPS or approximate location data
  • Camera or microphone access of any kind
  • Device contacts, calendar, or file storage
  • Payment or financial information (handled externally)
  • Biometric or health data
  • Browsing history outside this App
04

Technologies & SDKs Used

Our App is built with the following components, each with distinct data behaviour:

Android WebView Firebase Cloud Messaging Cloud Firestore Room Database Splash Screen API
Firebase (Google LLC) — FCM and Firestore are Google services. Firebase may collect device identifiers and operational data governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy. We use Firebase exclusively for notification delivery — not for analytics or advertising purposes.
Android WebView — Loads our restaurant website. May store session cookies locally, the same way a browser would. We do not inject scripts to harvest personal data. Clearing app data removes all WebView cookies and cache.
Room Database (on-device only) — Notification records (title, message, time) are stored in a local SQLite database via Android Room. This data never leaves your device and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.
Splash Screen API — Used only for the branded launch animation. It does not collect or transmit any data.
05

Push Notifications (FCM)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send notifications about your orders and restaurant updates. Your device is assigned an FCM token — a randomly generated identifier tied to the app install, not to your identity.

  • The FCM token is stored in Firestore solely to route notifications to your device
  • Tokens are rotated automatically by Firebase and refreshed on reinstall
  • You can disable notifications anytime via Android Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Notifications
  • Disabling notifications has no effect on your ability to browse or order
06

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only external service this App communicates with is Google Firebase (for FCM and Firestore), governed by Google's Terms of Service.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social login SDKs, third-party analytics tools, or any other external data processors beyond Firebase.

07

Data Retention

  • FCM Tokens (Firestore): Retained while the App is installed and notifications are enabled. Removed when you uninstall the App or revoke notification permission
  • Notification History (Room DB): On-device only. Persists until you clear app data or uninstall. We have no access to this data
  • Phone Numbers: Never stored — zero retention
  • WebView Cache / Cookies: Managed by Android's WebView engine and cleared when you clear app data
08

Security

We apply appropriate safeguards to protect the limited data we handle:

  • All App-to-server communication uses HTTPS / TLS encryption
  • Firestore security rules limit FCM token access to authorised server operations only
  • The Room database resides in the App's private storage, inaccessible to other apps
  • We do not store passwords, payment credentials, or sensitive personal data anywhere in the App

No digital system is entirely secure. If you discover a potential vulnerability, please contact us immediately using the details below.
